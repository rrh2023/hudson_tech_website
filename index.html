About

A freelance developer

I'm Roberto Rodas-Herndon, a Boston University Computer Science graduate & software developer. While at Boston University, I served as President of the Hack4Impact chapter (2021–2023), leading a student-run team that built free software for non-profits — the same mission that inspired the founding of HudsonTech.

From navigating Pinterest's codebase at their headquarters in San Francisco alongside senior engineers, to providing technical insights across the enterprise at PSEG, I bring big-company engineering rigor to organizations that need it most — those shaping their communities.

Every project ships with modern architecture and a focus on real-world impact. I'm also bilingual (English & Spanish), which helps me serve Hudson County's diverse communities directly in their language.
